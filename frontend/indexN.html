About us

At Abissinya Ethiopian Restaurant, we are dedicated to elevating the status and awareness of Ethiopian cuisine by fusing modern creativity with authentic delicious taste. The only thing better than our food is our impeccable service, through which we demonstrate warmth, professionalism, and integrity. Our main goal is to have you excited about coming back. Since 1999, Abissinya restaurant has been serving the finest Ethiopian cuisine in addisababa Ethiopia.
“Experience the taste of Ethiopia!”
